Music

Music is without a doubt one of the most important aspects of my life. In the past couple of years at MSU alone, I have undertaken a variety of musical projects, both commissioned by others as well as for my own enjoyment. Below is a sampling of some of the more noteworthy or interesting examples.


The video above is the MSU A capella group State of Fifths performing my vocal arrangement of the Imogen Heap song "Just for Now." While I have arranged several pieces for the ensemble, this was the only one to have been performed at one of their concerts. This particular video is from December 1, 2011.


This video is from the Spartan Marching Band halftime show at this year's football game vs. Notre Dame. From 1:07 onwards in the video, you can hear the custom dubstep remix of various Skrillex music I made for the band to choreograph. Being able to perform with the band, as well as your own custom music to a crowd of 80,000 is something I surely won't forget anytime soon.
